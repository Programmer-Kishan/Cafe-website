About Us

This is the place for all the pasta lovers in the world, and for people who no know that pasta is not only a dish it's a feel. The creamyness of that white sauce, tangyness of those tomatoes in Arrabiata, the sprinkle of organno, chilly flakes and many italian herbs, is only felt by the real pasta lovers.

We here at Delico give you a chance to show your pasta talent in front of the world. You can take part in our contest which are held every year and are also very fun. There are 3 contest which are held in a year and are judged by master chefs.
